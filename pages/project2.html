What You Will Do:

In this project, we will be describing and teaching you how to correctly put together a skateboard rack. This is a very simple project so long as you are okay working with everyday tools. At the end you will have the perfect place to hang all your longboards and skateboards!

Materials

Goto Navigation Get all your supplies here!
  • Drill with 1” bit
  • Jigsaw
  • 2 2-foot pieces of 1” X 8” Pinewood
  • 3 1” Hinges
  • Sand paper
  • Self applicating felt strips
  • Clamp (optional)

Setting Up the Project

Step 1:

Goto Navigation

Clamp the planks together

Following the diagram, drill holes in the planks

Using a jigsaw, connect the holes to the outside edge of the plank

Sand the rough edges

Building Your Skateboard Rack

Goto Navigation

Step 1:

Install the hard drive(s), making sure to plug the Sata cable into the hard drive. Push the hard drive(s) into the port until it clicks in place. The hard drives store data from the computer.

Lay the boards together so that the slots you cut out are lined up evenly.

Attach hinges at the top, middle, and bottom sections of the planks.

Apply the felt strips in order to prevent scratches to your skateboard(s).

Conclusion

That didn't take long! If you followed along correctly and did even step listed you should now have a skateboard rack that is ready to organize all your boards!
